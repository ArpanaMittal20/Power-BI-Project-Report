**Project Overview: Analyzing Historical IPL Data for Insights on IPL 2024 🏏
**


In this project, we analyzed IPL data from 2021-2023 to uncover insights for IPL 2024:

**🔍 Primary Insights:**

Top 10 batsmen and bowlers based on performance metrics.
Top teams based on winning percentages and chasing targets.

**📈 Secondary Insights:**

Predicted Orange and Purple Cap winners.
Estimated top four qualifying teams, winner, and runner-up.
Selected Best 11 players and top three all-rounders based on performance data


**🛠️ Tools used:** Power BI
